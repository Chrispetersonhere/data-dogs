# v1 Personas

## Independent Investor
- **Primary objective:** make confident, self-directed research decisions using verifiable filing evidence.
- **Typical tasks:** review company history, examine core financial trends, inspect source filings.
- **Needs from v1:** simple navigation, transparent lineage, and clear restatement history.

## Professor / Researcher
- **Primary objective:** run repeatable studies on U.S. public-company disclosures and accounting outcomes.
- **Typical tasks:** gather filing-linked facts, compare periods, export evidence-backed observations.
- **Needs from v1:** point-in-time-correct datasets, provenance metadata, and consistent fact definitions.

## Small Fund Analyst
- **Primary objective:** perform efficient pre-investment and ongoing diligence across a focused watchlist.
- **Typical tasks:** screen peers, validate unusual changes, cross-check compensation/insider signals.
- **Needs from v1:** reliable screener, fast filing drill-down, and trustable historical metric construction.

## RIA / Consultant
- **Primary objective:** produce client-ready research outputs with defensible sourcing.
- **Typical tasks:** prepare company briefings, compare peers, explain why figures changed over time.
- **Needs from v1:** auditable citation paths, concise workflows, and stable data quality.
