# v1 Non-Goals and Exclusions

The following are explicitly out of scope for v1:

1. Global ex-U.S. coverage.
2. Real-time market data, tick streaming, or intraday execution signals.
3. Trading, brokerage integration, or order management.
4. Claims of replacing CRSP or equivalent full historical databases.
5. AI-only extraction used as sole authoritative record.
6. Portfolio construction, optimization, or rebalancing engines.
7. Social feeds, chat rooms, or community sentiment features.
8. Consumer-grade financial planning workflows.
9. Native mobile app programs (iOS/Android) beyond web responsiveness.
10. Credit underwriting or risk scoring products.
11. Comprehensive macroeconomic data warehouse features.
12. Multi-asset support across fixed income, options, futures, or crypto.
