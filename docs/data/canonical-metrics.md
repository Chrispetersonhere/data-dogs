# Canonical Metrics Dictionary

Tight Month-1 dictionary. No metric sprawl.

| Key | Name | Description | Unit | Scope note |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `revenue` | Revenue | Top-line revenue recognized for the reporting period. | `currency` | Income statement flow metric for period performance. |
| `gross_profit` | Gross Profit | Revenue minus cost of goods/services sold. | `currency` | Income statement profitability before operating expenses. |
| `operating_income` | Operating Income | Profit from core operations before financing and taxes. | `currency` | Income statement operating performance metric. |
| `net_income` | Net Income | Bottom-line earnings after all expenses, interest, and taxes. | `currency` | Income statement final profit metric for the period. |
| `assets` | Assets | Total resources controlled by the entity at period end. | `currency` | Balance sheet stock metric at a point in time. |
| `liabilities` | Liabilities | Total obligations owed by the entity at period end. | `currency` | Balance sheet stock metric at a point in time. |
| `equity` | Equity | Residual interest in assets after deducting liabilities. | `currency` | Balance sheet stock metric for owners' claim. |
| `cfo` | Cash Flow from Operations (CFO) | Net cash generated by operating activities during the period. | `currency` | Cash flow statement operating section metric. |
| `capex` | Capital Expenditures (Capex) | Cash spent on long-lived productive assets. | `currency` | Cash flow investing outflow metric for reinvestment. |
| `fcf` | Free Cash Flow (FCF) | Operating cash flow minus capital expenditures. | `currency` | Derived cash generation metric (CFO - Capex). |
| `shares` | Shares Outstanding | Weighted or period-end shares used for per-share analytics. | `shares` | Capital structure quantity metric, not a currency amount. |
